Lab 05

Content Learned

This past class has been all about responsive design. Producing webpages that are more accessible to end-users, whether they are on a Laptop or Mobile device. This way they will be able to view all content that they need or want to see from use of that website.

The building blocks of responseive web design are essentially to a developer to produce a product that is appealing to the end-user. The building block include:

  • Usage of HTML and CSS to produce well organized displays of infomration and appealing design.
  • Usage of Media Queries to produce a product that is available to end-users on any device they might use for it. such as a smart phone or laptop device.
  • Usage of CSS structural Syntax such as Grid or Flex to create a usable and appealing layouts of your webpage

Through this assignment I learned how to use media queries to change the layout and diplay of a webpage to fit into multple devices based on the width and orientation of the device. producing accessible webpages that have different looks depending on the device. As well as the usage and trial and error of using grid or flex flows to arrange my webpage in different ways. producing an appealing outcome on my webpage.
